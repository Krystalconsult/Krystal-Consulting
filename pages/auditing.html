AUDITING.

Auditing is defined as the on-site verification activity, such as inspection or
examination, of a process or quality system, to ensure compliance to requirements.
Pre-certification (readiness) and internal audits - To determine readiness for external/
third party audits

  • ISO 9001:2015
  • Global and Local GAP
  • HACCP - SANS 10330:2020
  • FSSC 22000: V5.1
  • ISO 22000:2018
  • FSA

    • TYPES OF AUDITS

    First Party Audits

    On behalf of the business, normally performed by employees of the organization being audited however, external auditors may also be used.

    Second Party Audits

    Normally the supplier audits to ensure specified requirements stipulated by an agreement are being met.

    Third Party External Audits

    Performed by an external independent auditing firm or certification body.
